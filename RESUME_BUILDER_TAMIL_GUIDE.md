## 💡 Tips

### Resume-ஐ Better-ஆ Make பண்ண:

1. **Clear Objective**
   - Short and specific
   - Career goals mention பண்ணுங்க

2. **Skills Section**
   - Relevant skills மட்டும்
   - Technical + Soft skills

3. **Projects**
   - Technologies mention பண்ணுங்க
   - Impact/results சொல்லுங்க

4. **Achievements**
   - Certifications
   - Awards
   - Competitions

5. **Keep it Simple**
   - 1-2 pages max
   - Clear formatting
   - No spelling mistakes
