║                    HOW TO USE                                        ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

1. Login as student
2. Go to "Explore Resources"
3. Click "Resume Builder" tab
4. Select category and company (optional)
5. Choose a template
6. Fill in your details
7. Click "Download Resume"
8. Open HTML file in browser
9. Print to PDF (Ctrl+P → Save as PDF)
10. Share with recruiters!
